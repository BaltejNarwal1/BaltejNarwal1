Honolulu

Tourist Attractions

  • Waikiki Beach

    • See the sunset over Oahu on a 2-hour dinner cruise. Hop aboard the brand new vessel "The Majestic" and enjoy a relaxing evening on the waters off Waikiki.


  • Koko Crater

    • Test your endurance on an intense, 2-hour hike to one of the most beautiful spots in Oahu. Climb over 1,000 stairs to Koko Head Crater.


  • Daimond Head

    • Climb Hawaii’s most famous volcano on this 3-hour Diamond Head tour. A local guide will lead you through Diamond Head State Park.


  • Hanauma Bay

    • Spot schools of bright fish and other marine life on a half-day snorkeling adventure at Oahu’s Hanauma Bay.


  • Waikiki Trolley tour

    • Save up to 50% and enjoy unlimited Waikiki Trolley rides with entry to 14 fantastic on route attractions all for one low price. Enjoy watching dolphins at Sea Life Park Hawaii, take in epic views atop Diamond Head Crater, explore historic Pearl Harbor and much more.
